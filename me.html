I'm Harivarshan K A, currently pursuing M.Tech in Green Energy Technology at Pondicherry University after earning my B.Tech in Civil Engineering with a CGPA of 8.06 from Puducherry Technological University. I've combined my interest in sustainable engineering and creative design over the past few years. On the engineering side, I completed internships with Puducherry Smart City Development Limited, Puducherry Public Works Department, and Chennai Metro Rail Limited, gaining practical experience in urban infrastructure, water systems, and project planning. I'm also the founder of harrywhite, a design firm dedicated to making high-quality, exceptional design accessible to everyone, and a founding member of UNITY Foundation, which focuses on empowering youth and building stronger communities. I enjoy working on ideas that bring together technology, design, and sustainability to make a positive impact.
